Brickscape


Addictive 3D puzzle to wake up your brain
Move bricks to take out the Core Brick

How to play

Move Core Brick to the exit.
Every brick has to be moved in an elongated direction.
You can't move bricks out of the box.

Features

  • Meet more than 600 brain teasing stages in 12 aesthetic themes.
  • Simple and easy play for everyone.
  • 5 difficulty levels; from easy to master.
  • Choose your favorite level: You don't need to clear them in order.
  • Daily Puzzle Mode: Compete with other players around the world.
  • Unlike other puzzle games, there is no time limit.
  • Enjoy our brilliant and unique brick texture and sound design.
  • Ask for 'Hint' when needed, and use 'Undo' feature to correct mistakes.
  • No Internet connection required to play.
